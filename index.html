Calm. Bold. Kennko.

Slow mornings feel like a bold sip.

Kennko Cafe is the local lab for premium coffee and playful non-coffee drinks inside SM Butuan. Fresh beans, handcrafted coconut series, and cheerful humans ready to help you pause.

Locally rooted, globally brewed

Founder Kenn Baliling opened Kennko Cafe with a mission: give Butuan a coffee shop that felt like a boldly familiar hangout. Every espresso is pulled to highlight the blend of premium roasts and fresh Philippine ingredients.

From the Coconut Series to salted caramel oat lattes, the menu arrives with a pop of color, a dash of brightness, and a promise of community.
