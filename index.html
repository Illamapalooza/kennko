Kennko Cafe is where Philippine hospitality meets carefully curated specialty drinks. Come for the premium beans, stay for the coconut series and friendly baristas.

We are from Butuan

Founded in 2023 by Kenn Baliling, Kennko is the neighborhood cafe that leans on bold color, playful posture, and meticulous pour-overs. Expect premium coffee, cheeky snacks, and a vibe that feels like an instant smile.

Try our Coconut Series for tropical calm or the Salted Caramel Oat Latte when you want a sweet, creamy dash of comfort. Our kitchen also cooks up snacks that pair perfectly with every sip.
