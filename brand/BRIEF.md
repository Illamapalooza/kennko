# Kennko Coffee — Brand Brief

## About
Kennko Coffee is a cafe located inside SM Butuan City, Philippines. Founded in 2023 by Kenn Baliling. Known for premium coffee and non-coffee drinks in a fun, approachable atmosphere. The brand is bold, playful, and youthful.

## Menu

### Premium Coffee
- Americano
- Coffee Latte
- Spanish Latte
- Vanilla Latte
- White Choco Mocha
- Caramel Macchiato
- Vietnamese Coffee
- Egg Coffee

*We also serve hot.*

### Non-Coffee Drinks
- Signature Choco
- Matcha Latte
- Strawberry Milk
- Berry Matcha

### Specialty Series
- Coconut Series
- Salted Caramel Oat Latte
- Sea Salt Caramel Oat Latte
- Vanilla Milk Cloud Foam
- Strawberry Cloud Soda

### Perks Card
Loyalty program — collect stamps, get free upsize at 4 stamps and free drink at 8 stamps.

## Brand Colors
- **Primary:** #0055FF (bold electric blue) — used everywhere: backgrounds, logo, cards, menu
- **Secondary:** #FFFFFF (white) — text on blue, logo on white backgrounds
- **Accent:** #FFB830 (amber/yellow) — used sparingly for highlights like promo badges ("Free Upsize", "Free Drink")
- **Background options:** Blue (#0055FF) or White (#FFFFFF) — no dark/black themes

## Typography
- Logo font: Custom rounded/bubble style, bold, playful — all caps "KENNKO" with "COFFEE" below
- Body: Clean, rounded sans-serif to match the playful brand personality
- All headings in uppercase/bold

## Tone of Voice
- Fun, friendly, approachable
- Youthful and energetic
- Not corporate — casual and warm
- Tagline style: short, punchy phrases
